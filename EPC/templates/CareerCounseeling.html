Career Counseling

What is Career Counseling?

Career counseling is a process of helping individuals make informed decisions about their career path, based on their interests, skills, and abilities. At Employability Consultancy, our experienced career counselors will work with you to identify your career goals and help you develop a plan to achieve them.

Career Opportunities

Employability Consultancy provides information and resources to help individuals identify career opportunities based on their skills, interests, and qualifications. We also offer guidance on job applications and interview preparation to help you land your dream job.

Assessment Tests

Our career counselors will administer assessments to help you identify your strengths, skills, interests, and values. These assessments will provide valuable insight into the type of careers that may be a good fit for you.

Career Planning

Once you have identified your career goals, our career counselors will help you develop a plan to achieve them. We will provide guidance on education and training opportunities, as well as professional development and networking strategies to help you succeed in your chosen career.
